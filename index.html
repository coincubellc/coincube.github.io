Process

From Gold to Bitcoin in no time

Call for a quote

Call for a price indication quote. We will quote you a dollar amount relative to gold or silver spot price for each product you wish to sell. Once we have the relavant details for the bullion sale, we'll send you a quote by email.

Ship your metals to our warehouse

We will email you a packing slip which you will include in the bullion shipment. We recommend USPS because you can buy insurance of up to $50,000 per package when you send as Registered Mail.

Submit the shipment info

Send us the tracking number for the shipment by email or text.

Price locked

Once the shipment arrives at our warehouse, we will lock-in the sale price and send you a receipt with the total bitcoin due.

Set up Bitcoin wallet

We will work with you to set up and secure your Bitcoin wallet. We recommend Blockstream's Green Wallet. We'll walk you through generating a payment address from this wallet which you will submit to us to receive your bitcoin.

Receive your bitcoin

Once the bitcoin purchase has cleared with our exchange partners, we will send your bitcoin to the provided address. This usually takes ~2 business days.
